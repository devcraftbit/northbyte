Player Queries

Common questions regarding our titles, modding support, and platform availability.

Do your titles support community modding?
Absolutely. We believe games live longer and thrive when the community is empowered to build upon them. Official modding toolkits are released post-launch for our major single-player releases.
Are you planning to port your PC exclusives to consoles?
We develop our games with a PC-first mentality to push graphical and processing limits. However, our most successful and highly requested titles often see console iterations developed with trusted external partner studios.
How frequently are Developer Logs updated?
We aim for monthly deep dives into specific technical or artistic challenges, along with smaller weekly patch notes for our actively updated multiplayer environments.
