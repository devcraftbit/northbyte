Overhauling the Volumetric Render Pipeline

Behind the Scenes

The craft and tools behind our immersive worlds.

Capturing Realistic Movement

Our in-house motion capture facility allows us to prototype character animations rapidly. We utilize a 32-camera array to track full-body physics, capturing subtle weight shifts and momentum transfers.

This raw data is immediately piped into our custom engine tools, allowing animators to clean up and blend states within hours instead of days.

Foley and Sound Engineering

Audio is half the experience. We record thousands of practical sound effects using unconventional objects to build out our sci-fi weapon library and environmental impacts.

Every footstep is dynamically layered based on character weight, armor class, and terrain material, creating an unparalleled sense of physical presence.
